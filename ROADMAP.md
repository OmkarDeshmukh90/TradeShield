# Roadmap (Intentional Deferrals)

## Built in this showcase
- Multi-tenant auth/RBAC/audit.
- Worker-based ingestion with dedupe/backoff/source health.
- Deterministic demo replay mode.
- Explainability panel with factor breakdown and override delta.
- Approval/comment/outcome closed-loop workflow.
- `/ops` operational credibility page.

## Deferred intentionally
- Enterprise SSO/OIDC and SCIM.
- Distributed worker locking for multi-worker horizontal scale.
- Billing/subscription and plan enforcement.
- Full i18n/mobile-native app.
- Advanced contradictory-source analyst queueing.
- Deep ERP/TMS native integrations.

## Why deferred
- Showcase goal is depth and engineering judgment, not enterprise breadth.
- Current scope maximizes signal in architecture, reliability, and product execution flow.
