How It Works

This is the decision flow your team will follow each day.

  1. Detect: Global disruptions are collected automatically from trusted feeds.
  2. Understand: TradeShield maps each event to your suppliers, routes, and product groups.
  3. Decide: You get three practical options with trade-offs and confidence.
  4. Act: Assign owners, execute actions, and track outcomes.
